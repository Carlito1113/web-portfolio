This is Recommend

Carlito Algarin & Nicolas Fontan

Are you an avid reader? Have you ever hit a road block finding new books to read or that spark your interest?

My partner and I built Recommend as an application for book lovers to find and fairly review books of all varieties.

In Recommend you can review a book based on if you do or don't recommend it, followed by a summary of your thoughts on why this is the case. Simple as that, as a general user of Recommend, you can also search for new reads and save anything that sparks your interest to your account.
